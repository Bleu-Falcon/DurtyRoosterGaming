Apply to The Roost

Durty Rooster Gaming is not a public lobby. We’re a curated, 21+ community built on strong characters and better scenes.

If you’re looking for instant access, this probably isn’t for you. If you’re looking for quality roleplay and a solid group to build stories with — you’re in the right place.

Before You Apply

  • You are 21+
  • You understand semi-serious roleplay
  • You can separate in-character from out-of-character
  • You contribute to scenes, not just yourself

If that all sounds reasonable, hit the button below and tell us about yourself.
