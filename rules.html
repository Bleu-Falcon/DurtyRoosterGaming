The Roost Rules

Durty Rooster Gaming is a 21+ community built on semi-serious roleplay. We joke, we laugh, and we have fun — but we take the craft seriously.

1. Don’t Be a Problem

If you constantly cause issues, drama, or friction, this isn’t your place. Fun is mandatory. Chaos is not.

2. Roleplay With Intent

Scenes should have purpose. Characters should have personality. If you’re here just to grind or farm, you’re missing the point.

3. Respect the Scene

No powergaming. No metagaming. No main-character syndrome. Everyone matters — but no one is above the community.

4. 21+ Means Act Like It

Adult humor is fine. Immature behavior is not. We’re grown here. Handle yourself accordingly.

5. The Rooster Giveth. The Rooster Taketh Away.

Whitelisting is a privilege, not a right. If you lose it, you probably earned it.
