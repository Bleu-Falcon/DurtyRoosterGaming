Rules & Code of Conduct

Durty Rooster Gaming is a 21+ semi-serious roleplay community. These rules protect the quality of play and the people involved.

Respect Is Non-Negotiable

Edgy humor is welcome. Disrespect is not.

Roleplay Is a Craft

Stay in character when it matters. Break character intentionally, not disruptively.

21+ Community Standards

Adult themes may exist, but harassment and non-consensual behavior are never tolerated.

Whitelist & Enforcement

Quality over quantity. No one is irreplaceable. Behavior matters.

The Rooster giveth, and the Rooster taketh away.

Legal Notice

Participation is voluntary. Access may be revoked at any time to protect community integrity.
